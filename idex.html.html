Who we are

Science has a
perception problem.

STEMvia is a youth-led nonprofit making STEM feel less like a checklist and more like a world worth exploring. We cover the science in your skincare, the physics in your playlists, the biology in your backyard.

We lean into STEM entrepreneurship — the intersections where curiosity meets creation. Our content is for students who feel like science was never quite made for them, and for those who already love it and want to go further.

Because STEM isn't just med school and coding. It's designing the metaverse, saving coral reefs, and maybe even finding aliens.

Accessible, not dumbed down
We treat our audience as intelligent people who haven't been given the right entry point yet.
STEM as a way of thinking
Not a career path. A lens for everything — food, culture, entrepreneurship, daily life.
Youth-built, youth-first
We are students talking to students. No gap between who we are and who we're speaking to.
