Our story

We started with a question: why does STEM in India feel like a two-option test when the actual field is infinite?

Who we are

Young people who believe science should go further.

We're a nonprofit built and run by young people, we are students, tinkerers, first-time founders, curious minds.

But we're building something real, and we want you to be part of it from the ground up.

Why we exist

The road should have more than two lanes.

In India and across much of the world, STEM gets funnelled into two directions: become a doctor or become an engineer. That framing leaves out everything else — the researchers, the innovators, the entrepreneurs, the people building the next generation of solutions to climate, health, education, and economic inequality and so much more.

We're here to make the full picture visible. Because when young people see what STEM can actually become, they start building differently.

"A startup grounded in science isn't just a business idea. It's a hypothesis with a team behind it."
What we believe

Entrepreneurship is science meeting real life.

Startups can be more than a product or a pitch deck. When they're built on scientific thinking — understanding a problem deeply, testing ideas rigorously, iterating with evidence — they become something more powerful.

They become solutions. We want to put that kind of entrepreneurship in front of every young STEM mind we can reach.

Our reach

India-first.
Globally minded.

We're starting where we are — in India, where the STEM talent is enormous and the ecosystem is still catching up. But the problems we're talking about, and the community we're building, don't stop at a border. If you're anywhere in the world and this resonates, you belong here too.
